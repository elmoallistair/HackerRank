<b> PROBLEM </b>
> You are given two sets.<br>
> Set A = {1,2,3,4,5,6}<br>
> Set B = {2,3,4,5,6,7,8}<br>
>
> What is the total number of ordered pairs present in the Cartesian Product A &#9587; B?<br>

<b> ANSWER </b>
> _42_ <br><br>
