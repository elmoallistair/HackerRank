### PROBLEM
> You are given two sets.<br>
> Set **A** = {1,2,3,4,5,6}<br>
> Set **B** = {2,3,4,5,6,7,8}<br>
>
> What is the total number of ordered pairs present in the Cartesian Product _A &#9587; B?_<br>

### ANSWER
> 42
